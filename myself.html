Publications

AI-Driven Automated Quality Inspection for Beverage Bottles: Leveraging Object Detection Models for Enhanced Supply Chain Efficiency

International Journal of Innovative Science and Research Technology (IJISRT), Vol. 10, Issue 3, March 2025

Published a research paper on using AI-powered object detection to automate quality control in the beverage industry, focusing on enhancing supply chain efficiency. The paper explores how machine learning models can be deployed to identify defects in beverage bottles with high precision, reducing waste and improving production quality.

"This project addresses the critical need for maintaining product quality in the beverage industry by automating the inspection process. Utilizing advanced object detection models, the system identifies and classifies key defects such as cracked bottles, misaligned labels, missing caps, overfilled, underfilled, and normal bottles. Among the evaluated models, YOLOv8 demonstrated superior performance, achieving a 78% accuracy across all defect classes. By integrating this AI-driven quality control system into the supply chain, the approach minimizes human error, enhances operational efficiency, and ensures that only products meeting stringent quality standards reach consumers. This innovation not only reduces costs and waste but also significantly improves overall supply chain performance."
